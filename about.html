Our Story

From our humble beginnings with India’s first multiplex in 1997, we have revolutionized the way audiences experience cinema. Over the years, we have expanded across India, offering diverse viewing formats and delivering unforgettable moments to millions of movie lovers.

Milestones

1997 – First multiplex launched in Delhi
2006 – Company goes public
2012 – IMAX introduced to India
2023 – Merger with INOX creates India’s largest cinema chain
